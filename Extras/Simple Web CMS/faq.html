Frequently Asked Questions

. What do I have to do to play on this server?

1. Read the rules carefuly and make sure you understand them.

2. Register an account [ HERE ]

3. Make sure you have the official version 3.1.3 of the game installed.

4. Delete your WTF & CACHE folders from your WOW directory.

5. Replace your original file realmlist.wtf (found in WOW data\enUS (or your locale) directory) with the one found [ HERE ]

6. Play the game and enjoy !
